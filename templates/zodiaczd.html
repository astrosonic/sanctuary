Welcome to Sanctuary 3.0!

Sanctuary is a secure synchronous lightweight chatroom with zero logging and total transience.

Send Markdown-inspired messages

If you know Markdown, you are in for some exciting messaging experience here. Share formatted text contents, attach images and videos or add tables to what you are trying to convey easily using intuitive Markdown syntax and inbuilt parser! Learn more about how you can do so here

End-to-end encrypted conversations

With state-of-the-art client-side password-based Advanced Encryption Standard protection, your conversations are only readable by the ones who have the password. To add to it, no messages are stored or understood at the server end! Learn more about how you can do so here

Exclusive inclusion or inclusive exclusion?

We understand that not all messages in a group are intended for everyone present in the group. There can be certain messages you want to convey to a specific participant but hitching up an entire new chatroom can be inconvenient as hell! Learn more about how you can do so here

Insanely sanitized transmissions

We passionately learn from our mistakes and this time around, we ensured that no part of the input is transmitted without sanitization. This ensures protection against unscrupulous exploitation of security holes via provided inputs! Learn more about how you can do so here

Encountered a Persona Non Grata?

People can be very different (and very difficult at the same time). Although we suggest active inclusion of folks with different minds, there is nothing stopping you from getting rid of members should they turn things sour for the chatroom! Learn more about how you can do so here

Done and dusted with the talks

So you are done with having all the conversation you could in a chatroom and now want to put down the phone? You could either leave gracefully and we bid everyone a farewell for you or you could just close the tab but with no goodbyes! Learn more about how you can do so here

Uncompromising state of privacy

We understand that things could go terribly bad, should some of your private messages fall in the hands of someone whom you do not intend to. If you are paranoid enough, you can purge your chatroom and remove all messages and members! Learn more about how you can do so here

Awesomely subtle element of surprise

Anonymity can be comforting - especially when you get to drop messages to someone in your chatroom without them knowing that it was you who sent it. Of course, we advise discretion in the use of this feature as much as possible! Learn more about how you can do so here
